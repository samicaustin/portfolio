About Sami

I am a front-end software engineer, freelance artist, and an educator and mentor of 8+ years. I am a devoted lifelong learner who creates applications and sites that are beautiful, powerful, and user-centered. Most recently, I have expanded my creative and technical skills and capacity to support mission-driven projects through General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive course.

Over the course of my career, I have led over 300 students at four different campuses towards academic, personal, and career-oriented goals. My students have taught me how crucial continuous learning and development is; a value I carry with me no matter what project I pursue.
